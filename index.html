Mindlayer

On-device AI for Android

One AI model, shared with all your apps — running entirely on your phone. No cloud, no latency, no tracking.

Privacy Policy View on GitHub

Mindlayer is a background service that loads an on-device large language model once and serves AI inference — text generation, text embeddings, and optical character recognition — to your other apps over a private, on-device connection. It is built around a single principle: your data never leaves your device.

🔒 Fully offline

No internet permission, no servers, no remote APIs. Everything runs locally on your device.

🛡️ Private by design

No analytics, no tracking, no advertising. Nothing about you is collected or transmitted.

✅ You stay in control

Every app must be approved by you behind a biometric check before it can use the AI service.

⚡ One model, many apps

A single shared model instance serves all your apps efficiently, with thermal- and memory-aware scheduling.

For details on how your data is handled, read the Privacy Policy.
